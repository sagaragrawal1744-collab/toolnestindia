    Age Calculator

    Calculate your exact age in years, months and days.

    What is an Age Calculator?

    An age calculator helps you calculate your exact age from your date of birth. It shows your age in years, months and days.

    Why Use This Tool?

    • Calculate exact age instantly.
    • Useful for forms and applications.
    • Find age for school and government documents.
    • Completely free and easy to use.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How is age calculated?

    Age is calculated by comparing your date of birth with today's date and finding the difference in years, months, and days.

    Can I calculate age in months and days?

    Yes, this calculator provides your exact age in years, months, and days.

    Does this calculator consider leap years?

    Yes, leap years are automatically considered during the calculation.

    Can I calculate someone else's age?

    Yes, simply enter any date of birth to calculate that person's age.

    Is this age calculator free?

    Yes, this tool is completely free to use.

    Is my date of birth stored?

    No. All calculations happen in your browser and no information is stored.

    Can I use this for government forms?

    Yes, this calculator can help determine your exact age for forms and applications.
