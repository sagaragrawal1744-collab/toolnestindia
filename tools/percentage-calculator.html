    Percentage Calculator

    Calculate your percentage quickly and accurately.

    What is a Percentage Calculator?

    A Percentage Calculator helps you calculate the percentage of marks obtained in an exam or test instantly.

    Percentage Formula

    Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100

    Why Use This Tool?

    • Calculate exam results instantly.
    • Useful for school and college students.
    • Simple and accurate calculations.
    • Completely free to use.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I calculate percentage?

    Divide obtained marks by total marks and multiply by 100.

    Can I use decimal marks?

    Yes, decimal values are supported.

    Is this calculator free?

    Yes, it is completely free.

    Is my data stored?

    No. All calculations happen in your browser.

    Can I use it for college marks?

    Yes. It works for schools, colleges and competitive exams.

    Does it work on mobile?

    Yes, it is fully mobile responsive.
