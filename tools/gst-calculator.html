    GST Calculator

    Calculate GST amounts instantly with inclusive and exclusive modes.

    What is GST Calculator?

    GST Calculator helps you calculate Goods and Services Tax (GST) payable on products and services in India.

    What is GST?

    GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax imposed on the supply of goods and services in India.

    Why Use This Tool?

    • Calculate GST instantly.
    • Supports inclusive and exclusive GST.
    • Useful for businesses and freelancers.
    • Works for all major GST slabs.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is GST?

    GST stands for Goods and Services Tax introduced in India in 2017.

    What are the common GST rates?

    The common GST slabs are 5%, 12%, 18% and 28%.

    What is GST inclusive amount?

    Inclusive means GST is already included in the amount.

    What is GST exclusive amount?

    Exclusive means GST needs to be added to the amount.

    Is this calculator free?

    Yes, it is completely free.

    Is my data stored?

    No. Everything runs inside your browser.
