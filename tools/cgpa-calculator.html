    CGPA Calculator

    Enter your SGPA of each semester to calculate your overall CGPA.

    What is a CGPA Calculator?

    A CGPA Calculator helps students calculate their overall cumulative grade point average by averaging the SGPA of all semesters.

    How to Use?

    1. Enter your SGPA for each semester.
    2. Click Calculate CGPA.
    3. Your final CGPA will be shown instantly.

    Frequently Asked Questions
