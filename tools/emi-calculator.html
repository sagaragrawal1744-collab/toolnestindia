    EMI Calculator

    Calculate your monthly EMI, total interest and total payment instantly.

    What is an EMI Calculator?

    An EMI Calculator helps you calculate your monthly loan installment, total interest payable and total payment amount before taking a loan.

    Why Use an EMI Calculator?

    • Plan your finances better.
    • Compare different loans.
    • Know your monthly payments.
    • Calculate interest instantly.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is EMI?

    EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment paid every month towards a loan.

    How is EMI calculated?

    EMI is calculated using the loan amount, interest rate and loan tenure.

    Can I calculate home loan EMI?

    Yes, this calculator works for home, car and personal loans.

    Is this EMI calculator free?

    Yes, it is completely free.

    Can I use decimal interest rates?

    Yes, for example 8.5% or 9.25%.

    Does this calculator store my data?

    No. Everything runs in your browser.
