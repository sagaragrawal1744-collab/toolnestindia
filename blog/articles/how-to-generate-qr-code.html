How to Generate QR Codes for Free

QR codes allow users to instantly open websites, connect to WiFi, make payments and access information by scanning with a smartphone.

Popular Uses of QR Codes

  • Website Links
  • UPI Payments
  • WiFi Sharing
  • Business Cards
  • Event Registration

How to Create a QR Code

  1. Enter your text or URL.
  2. Click Generate.
  3. Download the QR code image.
