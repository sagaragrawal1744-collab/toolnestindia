How to Calculate GST

GST stands for Goods and Services Tax and is applied to most products and services in India.

GST Formula

GST Amount = (Original Price × GST Rate) ÷ 100

Example

Price = ₹10,000

GST Rate = 18%

GST Amount = ₹1,800

Final Price = ₹11,800

Common GST Rates

  • 5%
  • 12%
  • 18%
  • 28%
