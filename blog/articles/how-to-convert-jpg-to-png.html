How to Convert JPG to PNG

JPG and PNG are two of the most popular image formats. PNG supports transparency and often provides better quality for graphics and logos.

Why Convert JPG to PNG?

  • Support transparent backgrounds.
  • Better for logos and illustrations.
  • Lossless image quality.

How to Convert

  1. Select your JPG file.
  2. Click Convert.
  3. Download the PNG image.
