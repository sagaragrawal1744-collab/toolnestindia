How to Compress Images Without Losing Quality

Large images slow down websites and consume more storage. Image compression helps reduce file size while maintaining good quality.

Why Compress Images?

  • Faster websites
  • Lower storage usage
  • Quicker uploads
  • Better SEO performance

Popular Image Formats

  • JPEG
  • PNG
  • WEBP

Tips For Better Compression

  • Resize unnecessary large images.
  • Use WEBP format whenever possible.
  • Avoid compressing multiple times.
