How to Calculate Age

Age is the difference between your birth date and today's date.

How to Calculate Age Manually

Step 1: Note your birth date.

Step 2: Note today's date.

Step 3: Subtract years, months and days.

Example

Date of Birth: 15 June 2005

Today's Date: 28 June 2026

Age: 21 years and 13 days.

Why Age Calculation Is Important

  • School Admissions
  • Government Exams
  • Job Applications
  • Passport Verification
