How to Calculate EMI

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan.

EMI Depends On

  • Loan Amount
  • Interest Rate
  • Loan Tenure

Example

Loan Amount: ₹5,00,000
Interest Rate: 10% per year
Tenure: 5 Years

The monthly EMI will be approximately ₹10,624.

Where EMI Is Used

  • Home Loans
  • Car Loans
  • Education Loans
  • Personal Loans
