How to Calculate Percentage

Percentages are used everywhere including exams, discounts, business and finance.

Percentage Formula

Percentage = (Value ÷ Total Value) × 100

Example 1

A student scored 450 marks out of 500.

Percentage = (450 ÷ 500) × 100

Percentage = 90%

Example 2

You bought a ₹1000 product with a 20% discount.

Discount Amount = ₹200

Final Price = ₹800

Where Percentages Are Used

  • Examination Results
  • Bank Interest
  • Business Profits
  • Tax Calculations
  • Discounts
